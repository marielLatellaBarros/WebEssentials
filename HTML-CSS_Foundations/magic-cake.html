Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes in the world!
Few have tried and live! We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as result of consuming Magic Cake is not our responsibility. Please view our legal page for information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for an hour.
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
